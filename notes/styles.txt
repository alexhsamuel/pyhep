INDIVIDUAL AXES
---------------
x_axis_line		whether to draw the axis line
x_axis_thickness	thickness of the axis line
x_log_scale		log or linear scale
x_overflows		whether to draw overflow bins
x_axis_position		positioning of axis ("left", "right", "top", "bottom")
x_ticks			approximate number of ticks or list of tick positions
